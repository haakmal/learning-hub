# Design Fixation

This task asks you to reflect on your experiences generating and iteratively developing ideas for your assessments.

- **Reading:** Fast Co Design - What Is “Design Fixation,” And How Can You Stop It? - [https://www.fastcompany.com/3044535/what-is-design-fixation-and-how-can-you-stop-it](https://www.fastcompany.com/3044535/what-is-design-fixation-and-how-can-you-stop-it)

## Tasks

- Did you experience design fixation at any point?
- What are some strategies you used to overcome design fixation?
- Are there any other strategies that you would use next time to overcome fixation?
