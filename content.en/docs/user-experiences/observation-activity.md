#  Observation Activity

The purpose of these activities is to utilise insight from observation to further develop your prototype.

- **Reading:** Interaction Design - Preece, pg. 288-297, 8.6.1 Direct Observation in the Field, 8.6.2 Direct Observation in Controlled Environments

## Task

- Conduct an observation test on your most recent prototype with yourself as the user.
- Think aloud as you navigate your prototype and note down how you feel and behave.
- Reflect on the experience and note down any changes that need to be made to your idea.
- Brief a friend on the ‘Think Aloud’ technique of observation.
- Observe your friend using your prototype using the ‘Think Aloud’ technique.
- Document their observations and note any additional changes.
